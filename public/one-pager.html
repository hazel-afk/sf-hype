The Problem

Every recommendation platform—Yelp, Google, Amazon—makes money selling ads to the businesses they rank. Consumers can no longer tell what's genuinely popular from what's heavily promoted. AI-generated content is making it worse.

Our Solution

We analyze publicly observable signals—social mentions, review patterns, advertising footprints—to estimate the ratio of organic to paid visibility. High organic popularity = earned reputation. High paid visibility = manufactured reputation.
